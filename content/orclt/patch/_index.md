---

The `patch` command group integrates with **My Oracle Support (MOS)** to download Oracle patches, Release Updates, and bundled tooling. A valid MOS account is required.

MOS credentials can be provided as flags or saved to the encrypted keystore (see [Authentication](/orclt/getting-started/authentication/)).
